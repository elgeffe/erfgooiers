How to play

A cozy physical economy and military game — every plank, loaf and coin is carried automatically by hand. You control the action and strategy.
  • StoryOn Normal difficulty each level opens with a short briefing: a paragraph of the tale and exactly how to reach the goal. Click the objective card (top-right) any time to read it again. Turn these tutorials off in Settings or on the start-a-run panel; harder ascensions drop them automatically.
  • ChecklistOn the early Normal levels the objective card lists the buildings to construct and ticks them off as you build, and the next one to place glows in the build menu — follow the glow.
  • UnlockOn Normal the build menu starts small and unlocks a few buildings each level — locked cards show a 🔒 and are greyed out. The top resource bar shows only what you can currently make, so there’s less to take in at first. From level 5 the whole menu is open.
  • BuildPlace buildings from the bottom menu. Serfs (Transport Units) haul timber & stone from your castle to the site, then a laborer constructs it.
  • WatchVillagers become Specialists, they gather and craft; serfs automatically carry goods between buildings. Nothing teleports.
  • RoadsPaint roads for 1 stone each — all workers prefer them and walk 30% faster on them.
  • PlotsFarms, vineyards & pig farms need plots — open the building by clicking on it, then use the Plot tool to add crop & pasture tiles closely around them (each building has a plot limit); use demolish to clear them.
  • ChainsTrees → trunks → timber · Farm → mill → bakery (bread) · Vineyard → winery (wine) · Pig farm → butchery (sausage) · Gold + coal → the Mint (coins) · Fishery nets fish from the lake shore.
  • GoldGold piles glint in the grass — send any unit (or your hero) by right clicking (use shift+right click / cmd+right click to chain commands) over one and it is scooped straight into your purse.
  • WarIron mine → weaponsmith (weapons) & armorer (armor) → the Barracks trains soldiers, archers & knights. Watchtowers shoot enemies on their own.
  • ArmyDrag a box over fighters to select them · right-click to move or attack (use shift+right click / cmd+right click to chain commands) · right-click an enemy wall, gate, or stronghold to besiege it · Shift+1–5 saves a squad, 1–5 recalls it · right-click with a barracks selected plants its rally flag.
  • RunClear each level to reach the shop; a failed level ends the run. Debug “✓ Win” advances a level for now.
  • ControlsWASD / arrows or right-drag to pan · wheel to zoom · R rotates a building while placing · click a building to inspect · Esc cancels.
