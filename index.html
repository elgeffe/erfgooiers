How to play

A cozy physical economy — every plank, loaf and coin is carried by hand.
  • BuildPlace buildings from the bottom menu. Serfs haul timber & stone from your storehouse to the site, then a laborer constructs it.
  • WatchSpecialists gather and craft; serfs automatically carry goods between buildings. Nothing teleports.
  • RoadsPaint roads for 1 stone each — workers prefer them and walk 30% faster on them.
  • PlotsFarms, vineyards & pig farms need plots — use the Plot tool to add crop & pasture tiles around them (each building has a plot limit); demolish to clear them.
  • ChainsTrees → trunks → timber · Farm → mill → bakery (bread) · Vineyard → winery (wine) · Pig farm → butchery (sausage) · Gold + coal → the Mint (coins) · Fishery nets fish from the lake shore.
  • GoldClick gold piles glinting in the grass to collect them straight into your purse.
  • WarIron mine → weaponsmith (weapons) & armorer (armor) → the Barracks trains soldiers, archers & knights. Watchtowers shoot raiders on their own.
  • ArmyDrag a box over fighters to select them · right-click to attack or move (they fan out) · Shift+1–5 saves a squad, 1–5 recalls it · right-click with a barracks selected plants its rally flag.
  • RunClear each level to reach the shop; a failed level ends the run. Debug “✓ Win” advances a level for now.
  • ControlsWASD / arrows or right-drag to pan · wheel to zoom · R rotates a building while placing · click a building to inspect · Esc cancels.
